# BOB
It's your personal voice assistant
A project to build an AI voice assistant using Python . The Voice assistant interacts with the humans to perform basic tasks.



Bob is an AI personal voice assistant service built using Pychram. It can understand human speech and perform basic task designed by the client.

When the user specify the appropriate trigger words , The Bob gets activated and executes the user commands.

Bob AI Voice assistant:"Loading your personal Assistant Bob.... Hello, Good Morning" (Greets the user according to time)

The implemented Voice assistant can perform the following tasks:

Opens a wepage : Youtube , Play music , Google Chrome , StackOverflow

Human : Hey Bob, Open Youtube


Predicts time

Human : Hey Bob , What is the time


3.Fetch Top headlines from Times of India

Human:Hey Bob , what's the latest news?


Captures a photo

Human:Hey Bob, Take a photo


Searches data from web

Human: Hey Bob, Search Butterfly images from web


Ask geographical and computational questions

Human: Hey Bob, What is the capital of California? / Hey Bob what is Sin 90?


Predict Weather of different Cities

Human: Hey Bob , What is the weather likely now in Kerala?


Abstarct necessary information from wikipedia

Human: Hey Bob , Who is Bill Gates according to Wikipedia


The voice assistant abstarcts first 3 lines of wikipedia and gives the information to the user.

Ask Bob to introduce himself

Human: Hey Bob, Who created you? / Hey Bob , What can you do


10.Turn off your pc when required

Human: Hey Bob , Please turn off my PC
